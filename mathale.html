Mathale

Sri Lanka is a country which never fails to surprise it travelers with its incredible scenic beauty, lush green landscapes and the historic reminiscents which adds a touch of history. This hill country offers a warm climate throughout the year and of course the tropical beaches never fail to surprise. Apart from that there are ancient forts, incredible safaris where one can witness an abundance of animals and the rich traditions which offers a beautiful and vast culture, this magnificent country has all within one boundary. While there are many places which binds up the entire country, and one of them is Matale which is located in the Central province of Sri Lanka. Located at a distance of 142 kilometres from Colombo and Kandy, this place is a hidden gem which is away from the sight of the tourists. Enclosed by the Knuckles Mountain Range, there are many things which can be experienced at this place. Alongside this there are some handpicked places to visit in Matale where people can enjoy the beauty of the landscapes and also explore the heritage of the town. From temples to exploring the hiking trails, there is a long list of attractions which cannot be missed while at this region.

1. Muthumariamman Temple

Muthumariamman temple is also called the Arulmigu Sri Muthumari Amman Kovil which is actually an ancient temple dedicated to the Mariamman, the goddess of rain and fertility. Boasting a magnificent and colorful architecture with tall towers, there are intricate carvings and beautiful statues of god and goddesses.

2. Wasgamuwa National Park

Home to elephants, leopards and many more animals, the Wasgamuwa National Park is a must-visit for the people who want to explore Sri Lanka's wildlife. Situated at a distance of 225 kms from the Colombo city, this park is known for being a home to a vast number of Sri Lankan elephants. This place has around 140 species of birds and 150 floral species which will definitely fascinate you.

3. Knuckles Mountain Range

This is one of the top places to visit in Kandy Resembling the shape of a knuckle of a clenched fist from the top, Knuckles Mountain Range is probably one of the most scenic visiting places in Kandy. The mountain Range in the region towards the northern end of Sri Lankan highlands (39 km from Kandy) is the place to be. Perfect place to try your hands on adventure traveling and camping, the Knuckles Mountain Range spans from Matale to Kandy and is a treat for the nature lovers. Look around and embrace the wilderness brought to you by sprawling grasslands, jagged peaks, torrents of streams, and gushing waterfalls. Enjoy the series of painted canvas around.

4. Riverton Gap

A trip to the Riverton Gap in Sri Lanka: This place is located a few kilometers away from the Matale town.It takes a few hours to reach from Kandy. The main attractions of this place include the scenic beauty and trekking. The two spectacular waterfalls present here, Sera Ella and Bambarakiri Ella. To reach the waterfalls one must trek through the marvelous forest and witness the beauty.
