Mullaitivu

Mullaitivu's future is bright, despite its tough history! This region is being renovated and will provide excellent eco-tourism possibilities. The beaches surrounding Mullaitivu are peaceful and beautiful. It's just a matter of time until tourism returns to Mullaitivu, bringing new life to this peaceful and lovely island.

1. Elephant Pass Monument

It may take about 1 hour to go from Jaffna to the important Elephant Pass area, linking the Jaffna peninsula to the mainland. Several conflicts in the past demonstrated the significance of this location. A handful of military memorials may be found on each side of the road nearby.

2. Mullaitivu beach

The town is located between the sea and a lagoon. This whole region was devastated by the tsunami, and then the harshest, last stage of the conflict produced rampages and reduced it to ruins. It's great to witness the changes that are taking place and the brave societies at least once. Even if there aren't many facilities, there is a chance to go for a quick swim.
